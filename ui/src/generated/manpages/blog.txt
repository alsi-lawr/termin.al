NAME
     blog - Open blog content.

SYNOPSIS
     blog

DESCRIPTION
     Opens  the  published  entries beneath ~/blog as a hierarchical collection.
     Publication titles, dates and collection paths come  from  validated  meta‐
     data; selecting an entry opens its rendered document and returning restores
     the collection selection.

VIEWER
     Use  j/k  or  the arrows to move, h/l or left/right to collapse and expand,
     g/G or Home/End to jump, Enter to toggle a branch or open an entry,  and  /
     to  filter.  Escape  leaves the filter input first; from the collection, q,
     Escape or Ctrl+C returns to the owning shell. Mobile controls expose  move‐
     ment, filtering, opening and Shell return.

FAILURES
     Extra  arguments are rejected. Missing or misclassified publication content
     produces the  stable  content-unavailable  diagnostic.  Cancellation  opens
     nothing.

LIMITS
     Collection loading traverses at most 100 virtual entries to a maximum depth
     of 100. The bounded collection does not read outside ~/blog.

NOTES
     The  collection and opened documents are transient pane presentation. Clos‐
     ing them preserves the shell transcript, history and working directory.

EXAMPLES
     $ blog
