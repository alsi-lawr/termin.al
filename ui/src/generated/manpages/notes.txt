NAME
     notes - Open notes content.

SYNOPSIS
     notes

DESCRIPTION
     Opens validated note publications beneath ~/notes as a hierarchical collec‐
     tion.  Selecting a row opens its rendered Markdown document; returning from
     the document restores the collection and its selection.

VIEWER
     Use j/k or arrows to move, h/l or left/right to collapse and expand, g/G or
     Home/End to jump, Enter to toggle/open, and / to filter. Escape leaves fil‐
     ter editing first. From the collection, q, Escape or Ctrl+C returns to  the
     shell.  Mobile  controls  provide  movement, Filter/Clear, Toggle, Open and
     Shell.

FAILURES
     Extra arguments are rejected. Missing or misclassified  note  content  pro‐
     duces  the  stable content-unavailable diagnostic. Cancellation opens noth‐
     ing.

LIMITS
     Loading traverses at most 100 virtual entries to a maximum depth of 100 be‐
     neath ~/notes.

NOTES
     The collection is read-only and transient. Closing it preserves the  owning
     pane's transcript, history and working directory.

EXAMPLES
     $ notes

SEE ALSO
     blog(1), projects(1), open(1)
